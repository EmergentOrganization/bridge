Planiverse Bridge

Infinite universes, one bridge.

About

Planiverse Bridge connects you to a dimension in peril. You'll explore what remains of the intricate world which you have been brought to by a scientist, who --- against popular opinion --- has reached out into the multiverse for help. Matter in this plane of existence is being enveloped by an outbreak of unknown origin known as the Vyroids, and the technological creatures that inhabit this planiverse will not fight it. Can you help stop the vyroids?
